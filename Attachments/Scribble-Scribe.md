# Opening
You felt your consciousness soaring across landscapes and valleys, settling on a small city. You recognize it as [[Clovermore]]. Your mind focuses on a dark room inside a lavish building, where three featureless humanoid figures sit. “Seek these three.” A disembodied voice, your patron, urges. “They hold dangerous knowledge… It must be guarded.”  
You wandered the city looking for the ornate building from your dream. Kenku are not common in these parts, so you attract a few unwanted eyes as you traverse. Eventually, you approach what you think might be the building from your dream. You try to walk up to it and are stopped by two hulking guards. They ask what you’re doing here. You try to explain away your presence, just looking around, but they aren’t convinced. Before you can react, you feel something strike the back of your head and you are swallowed by darkness.
# Backstory
After his flock is ousted by the local [[Theives Guild]] after it was raided due to the sloppy work of the hired kenku hands, Scribble-Scribe scoured the ruins of the guild building hoping to find any valuables or knowledge that could help pay off the debt his flock has incurred. In doing so, he summoned an entity of unfathomable knowledge. He begged the entity for the knowledge to save his flock from oblivion and in return, he would give his service and fealty. The deal was struck, and with his flock saved, he now wanders the world alone, gleaning all the secrets of the world, both ancient and young.
