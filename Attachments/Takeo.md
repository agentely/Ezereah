Takeo was a soldier during the red apple rebellion. Upon the rebellions end the man who became head of the newly formed red apple house was left with few advisors, as many of the men who would have been advisors died before the end of the rebellion. the lord of the house was forced to take men of the old regime. This lead to the changes made by the new lord to be half measures and superficial. Takeo realized this the night he and his men were sent to kill a family that spoke to load of being hungry. the family was killed by his men and he killed them in return. He left his land with only the armor on his back and the sword at his side. He ran till the land change and the people spoke different than any he met. With no money and a hungry belly he became a bounty hunter. He became successful enough to by a priest and hired to hunt down [[Calieus au Vespien]]. this foe he was sent to kill is a great hunter someone whom my prove Takeo's end someone whom if he should kill may restore some honor.  
[[House of the Red Apple]] took over his home after the rebellions ended. The new lord sent Takeo and his army to kill a family and his army died in the fight as well. Being the only survivor, he fled in shame.
