Patron is [[Unnamed Patron]]
After his flock is ousted by the local [[Theives Guild]] after it was raided due to the sloppy work of the hired kenku hands, Scribble-Scribe scoured the ruins of the guild building hoping to find any valuables or knowledge that could help pay off the debt his flock has incurred. In doing so, he summoned an entity of unfathomable knowledge. He begged the entity for the knowledge to save his flock from oblivion and in return, he would give his service and fealty. The deal was struck, and with his flock saved, he now wanders the world alone, gleaning all the secrets of the world, both ancient and young.
