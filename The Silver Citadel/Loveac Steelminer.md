Loveac is an aspiring poet who owns an apothecary. By day he’s a lowly shopkeep, by night he gathers information to take down [[The Enlightened Monastery]]. He wants to take down the monastery because they began extorting his business. He’s forced to pay the monastery to sell knowledge.
